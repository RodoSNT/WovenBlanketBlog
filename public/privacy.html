Privacy Policy

Mapacito Designs Pin Publisher — Last updated: March 26, 2026

This privacy policy describes how Mapacito Designs Pin Publisher ("the App"), operated by Mapacito Designs ("we", "us"), collects, uses, and protects information when you use our application.

1. What This App Does

Mapacito Designs Pin Publisher is a personal automation tool that publishes product pins from our Etsy woven blanket shop to our own Pinterest business account. The App is used exclusively by the business owner for managing pins, boards, and analytics on a single Pinterest account. It does not serve third-party users.

2. Pinterest API Usage

This App uses the Pinterest API v5 to perform the following actions on the connected Pinterest account:

  • Read account profile information
  • Read and create boards
  • Read and create pins
  • Access analytics data for published pins

This App is not endorsed, certified, or otherwise approved by Pinterest. Pinterest is a registered trademark of Pinterest, Inc. Our use of the Pinterest API is governed by the Pinterest Developer Guidelines and Pinterest Terms of Service.

3. Information We Collect

When you connect your Pinterest account to this App via OAuth 2.0, we receive:

  • Access token and refresh token — used to authenticate API requests on your behalf
  • Account username and account type — displayed in the App dashboard
  • Board names and IDs — used to assign pins to the correct boards

We do not collect passwords, personal messages, follower lists, or any information beyond what is necessary to publish pins and view analytics.

4. How We Use Your Information

Information received from the Pinterest API is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate and maintain the connection to your Pinterest account
  • Create and manage pins and boards on your account
  • View pin performance analytics

We do not sell, share, rent, or redistribute any Pinterest data to third parties.

5. Data Storage and Security

Access tokens are stored locally on the business owner's computer in an encrypted configuration file. Tokens are not transmitted to any external servers, cloud services, or third parties. All API communication occurs directly between the App and Pinterest's API servers over HTTPS.

6. Data Deletion

Upon disconnecting your Pinterest account from this App, all stored access tokens, refresh tokens, and cached Pinterest data (board IDs, pin IDs, analytics data) are immediately and permanently deleted from local storage. No Pinterest data is retained after disconnection.

You may disconnect your account at any time through the App's dashboard or by revoking access in your Pinterest security settings.

7. Third-Party Services

This App interacts only with the following external services:

  • Pinterest API (api.pinterest.com) — for pin and board management
  • Etsy (etsy.com) — destination URLs for pins link to our Etsy shop listings

No other third-party services receive your Pinterest data.

8. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at individuals under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
